NeighborBoost

The Challenge

Design a third-party app for Android mobile devices that highlight peopleâ€™s favourite local and undiscovered small businesses. Created as an entry to the 2021 Adobe Jam design contest.

The Problem

Many new small businesses are popping up across the city, but they do not yet have a strong customer base. Users want to try out new businesses but are not sure what to expect. The app must increase revenue for the businesses and simplify finding a new local favourite for users.

The Solution

NeighborBoost allows individuals to discover new businesses based on type of business, location, and reviews from neighbors. Small businesses partner with the app to grant exclusive discounts to neighborboost users. Users are shown businesses based on how many boosts a business has, how close it is, and if any discounts are available. Users are able to use the app to find small businesses, read reviews, and get directions to the location.

Persona

Beth
  • Behaviours
  • Goes out for coffee most mornings
  • Looking for new regular spot
  • Has limited budget
  • Frustrations
  • If a new place is bad it feels like she has wasted money
  • Discovering new coffee places takes a lot of research and time
  • Overwhelmed by options
NeighborBoost will allow Beth to discover new coffee spots easily and with limited risk. She knows whatever place she chooses has good revoiew from her neighbor, and may have a deal for finding it through the app.
